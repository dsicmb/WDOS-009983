PAMPER THE PANDA ...

Dehiwala Zoo is the only authorised home for Pandas in Sri Lanka . Our in house Pandas are extremely precious to us & we cater ever need meticulously .They have very similar, but separate, enclosures here at the zoo as in the wild giant pandas are solitary animals that only meet once a year during the breeding season.

Since coming from China our pandas and have settled in really well into this more peaceful and natural location at the zoo. Their large outdoor areas have lots of trees, large wooden climbing frames and ponds for them to explore, play and snooze on.Each panda also has an off show area where keepers weigh the pandas each morning, carry out important daily training, and allow them to choose to spend time away from visitors if they need some peace and quiet.Please be aware indoor and outdoor viewing for any of our animals may be closed at short notice for health checks and welfare reasons ...

FARM-ANIMAL BARN ...

Are you looking for some fun activities to enjoy with the kids in the beautiful Dehiwala Zoo ? The Farm animal Barn is a unique blend of a children's farmyard and wildlife park. You can hold, touch and feed most of our animals in complete safety and at your own comfort level. And we pride ourselves in providing the best one of the best experiences for families . RIDING THE PONIES & HORSES ...

Pony & horse riding at Dehiwala Zoo is a great way to bring the families together and escape from the hustle & bustle of city life. Inspire a lifelong love of ponies & horses in your little buckaroo with a safe and fun, hand-led pony & horse ride. We have friendly ponies available speacilly for children 2- 6 years old who weigh less than 75 pounds. Our pony rides are specially designed for nervous, first-time riders and consist of three laps around a corral with an experienced guide. Got a herd of kids? This is a great group activity with plenty of photo ops for parents and grandparents.

OUR RAINBOW PARADISE ...

Our colourful Parrot Paradise , the rainbow universe , takes you on a trip to South America & Australia to discover ecosystems as rich as they are fragile. Through the immersive routes of the rainforests of amazon you will meet delightful macaws & parrots , often threatened with extinction in their natural habitats are safe & sound under their new home as our island itself is a tropical paradise which is enriched enough for them to thrive under our protection. Parrot Paradise works hand in hand with the Parrot Wildlife Foundation, an organization that participates in the conservation and reproduction of endangered psittacidae, as well as all species present in their natural habitats. Thanks to the expertise of caretakers and veterinarians, some species present in the park are also the subject of breeding programs, overseen by the European Association of Zoos and Aquariums, in order to avoid their disappearance if they were to occur. extinguish in the wild.
